Meals By Grace - Student Ambassador

Meals by Grace is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fighting food insecurity in our community by providing free groceries and essential supplies to thousands of families across the city and county. I've volunteered there for over 150 hours, and for much of that time, I've led a subteam focused on organizing and distributing detergent and household essentials. Leading this team taught me how to coordinate with others, manage inventory efficiently, and ensure that every family gets what they need—there's real responsibility in making sure operations run smoothly so no one leaves empty-handed.

What makes Meals by Grace special is the sense of dignity and care they bring to their work. It's not just about logistics; it's about building connections and offering reassurance to people during difficult times. This experience has shaped how I approach teamwork, organization, and leadership in every other part of my life.
